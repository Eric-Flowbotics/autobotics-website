The Leak
$1,680/mo
3 missed calls a week × $140 average job × 4 weeks

Tuesday, 2pm. You're elbow-deep in a job when the phone buzzes — by the time you check it, there's a voicemail from a number you don't know. And most of those callers are already dialing the next company on the list: 85% of people who hit a voicemail never call back.

Stat: industry research across 875,000+ U.S. cleaning businesses
The Fix

Missed-call text-back: anyone who calls and doesn't reach you gets a text within seconds. They know you exist, they know a quote is coming — and they stop dialing your competitor.

  1. 01Write your message. Take the one on the card below word-for-word, or make it sound like you.
  2. 02Set the trigger: every missed call during work hours. After-hours gets its own message — that's a future issue. One leak at a time.
  3. 03Turn it on for your line. Every phone hides this setting somewhere different — the second card below walks you through yours, step by step.
  4. 04Test it. Have someone call you and hang up. The text should land before they've pocketed their phone.
  5. 05Tally it. Keep a note on the dash — every reply your text-back catches this week gets a mark.
  6. 06Friday, do the math. Marks × your average job = what this fix caught in week one. That number goes on the fridge.
