Straight Questions

Straight questions, straight answers.

Is this actually free?

Yes. The newsletter, the Learn Library, and the Revenue Leak Score all cost nothing — no card, no catch. I'd rather earn your trust in public than sell you something you're not sure about yet. Paid options come later, and the free stuff stays genuinely useful — never bait.

I'm not a tech person.

You don't need to be. If you can learn a new piece of equipment, you can do this. Every fix comes as plain-language steps and prompts you copy and paste — no code, no jargon. I handle the technical part so you don't have to.

Will this work for my trade?

If you take calls, send quotes, book jobs, and chase invoices, yes. Cleaners, landscapers, HVAC, plumbers, handymen — the leaks are the same shape across home services, and so are the fixes. Where a trade needs its own example, I give it one.

Isn't this just more AI hype?

Most of what's out there is. I won't point you at a tool or a tactic I haven't used or verified myself, and I'll never quote you a result I can't stand behind. If something isn't worth your time, I'll say so.

How much time does this take?

The newsletter is one email a week, about five minutes. The Leak Score is three minutes on your phone. Each fix is built to set up once and then run in the background — the point is to give you your evenings back, not hand you another project.
