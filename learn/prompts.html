Answer & capture the call

Set up missed-call text-back on your phone (no CRM)

Use it when: you don't run a CRM and you want your phone to auto-text a missed call — this finds your phone's options and walks you through the setup.

Prompt 
I own a home-services business. My phone is a [phone model]. I want a text sent automatically when I miss a call. Walk me through every option my phone has. For each one, tell me whether it's fully hands-free, whether it requires me to tap anything, whether it silences my other notifications, and whether it's reliable enough for business use. Then give me step-by-step setup instructions.

Quote fast, follow up

Write your quote follow-up ladder (day 1 / 3 / 7)

Use it when: a quote's gone quiet and you want three friendly, no-pressure follow-ups on a schedule — plus the phone reminders so you actually send them.

Prompt 
I own a home-services business. I sent a customer a quote for [job] and haven't heard back. Write me three short, friendly follow-up texts I can send — one the day after, one three days later, one a week later. Keep them warm and human, not pushy. Then tell me, step by step, how to set reminders on a [phone model] so I actually send them on time.
