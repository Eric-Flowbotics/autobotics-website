About

I'm Eric. I translate AI for people who do real work.

You know the guy who fixes your AC at 2pm in July, or the woman who's cleaned your house every other Thursday for three years? Those people are incredible at what they do. But nobody taught them how to run a business — and now everyone's telling them AI will change everything, in a language nobody bothered to translate. That's the job I've given myself.

The short version of me

I went into the Army at 17 — to Iraq twice, out just before my 21st birthday. The sixteen years since: building products for companies you'd recognize, a company I co-founded that won a Department of Veterans Affairs Industry Innovation Competition, and a day job that's now all-in on AI. I'm not new to any of this. I'm just new to telling you about it.

What Autobotics is

Autobotics is a weekly newsletter (The Home Service Edge), a YouTube channel, a free operator community, and live workshops — all focused on one question: how does a home service business stop leaking money with AI? It's built for operators who want to learn this and run it themselves. And for the ones who'd rather have it done for them — that path is coming too, and this list hears about it first.

The bet I'm making

Most people giving AI advice have never run anything on it. So I'm building a home services company — designed from day one to run on AI: the phones, the quotes, the follow-ups, the books. It isn't operating yet, and that's deliberate — you get the build, not the highlight reel. The documentation starts now: every system, every cost, every failure goes into the newsletter and the channel as it happens.

One promise, and it's the whole brand

I will never sell you an outcome I haven't produced. When I've done the thing, I'll say so. When I'm showing you something that worked for someone else, I'll name where it came from. Sixteen years of building taught me that trust compounds faster than hype.
