ABOUT AUTOBOTICS

These are good people doing hard work. Nobody's helping them run the business side.

We built a real home service business with AI. Proved it works. Now we help other operators do the same thing — starting at whatever they can afford.

Eric Lenhardt · Founder

I didn't start this to build a tech company. I started it because I kept meeting people who deserved better.

I served two tours in Iraq before I was 20. That's where I learned what it looks like when people do hard work under hard conditions — and what happens when they don't have the right support behind them.

After the Army, I spent 20 years building products and companies. I co-founded Potentia Labs, a startup that won a contract with the VA Center for Innovation to build software helping veterans navigate PTSD and depression. I'm a product manager by trade — figuring out what actually works is literally my job.

When I started Lenny's Cleaning, it wasn't because I wanted to be in the cleaning business. It was because I wanted to prove something: could I use AI to automate every part of the business that kills solo operators? The missed calls. The quotes that never get followed up. The reviews that never get asked for. The customers who disappear because nobody reaches back out.

For $214 in tools, I built systems that answer the phone, send quotes same-day, follow up automatically, collect reviews without asking, and rebook customers on a schedule. The stuff that normally requires a $3,500/month office manager — automated, running, and documented.

Now I help other operators do the same thing. Not with theory. Not with a course. With the exact systems I built and tested on a real business — adapted to yours.
